Building Fund

Team 60 has started a project to build a community robotics center on Kingman High School's campus. This building will provide a space for our team to work on, machine, program, and test robots. It will also provide a space that other community robotics programs can use.

Currently the project has been started, but we are still raising additional funding. Donations can be made directly to our account at Foothills bank or sent to Kingman Robotics at PO Box 6492 Kingman, AZ.

For more information, please contact us at firstteam60robotics@gmail.com
