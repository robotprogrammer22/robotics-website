Crescendo

The challenge for the 2024 FIRST Robotics Competition season is called Crescendo. We will receive the information for the 2024 game on January 6, 2024.

A teaser video for the FIRST IN SHOW season was released at the end of the 2023 World Championships. In addition to the FRC Crescendo announcement, the names and dates of the challenges for FIRST Lego League (Masterpiece, releasing August 1, 2023) and FIRST Tech Challenge (Centerstage, releasing Septermber 9, 2023) were also announced.

More information about the season can be found on FIRST's website
