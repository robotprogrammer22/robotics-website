2025 - Reefscape

Reefscape

The challenge for the 2025 FIRST Robotics Competition season is called Reefscape. This challenge is part of the FIRST Dive season, which are water/ocean themed robotics challenges. We received our new challenge January 4, 2025. Then we will have approximately 6-8 weeks to design, prototype, build, and program a robot before going to competition in March and April. See the video below for the animation video describing this year's game.

This year we competed at the Arizona North Regional and the Las Vegas Regional.
At Arizona North we finished at rank 17.

At the Las Vegas Regional we won the event along with our alliance partners 987, the Highrollers, and 4414, High Tide. We also won the Gracious Professionalism Award.
