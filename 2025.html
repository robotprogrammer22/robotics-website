Reefscape

The challenge for the 2025 FIRST Robotics Competition season is called Reefscape. This challenge is part of the FIRST Dive season, which are water/ocean themed robotics challenges. We will receive our new challengeon January 4, 2025. Then we will have approximately 6-8 weeks to design, prototype, build, and program a robot before going to competition in March and April.

This year we will be competing at the Arizona North Regional (March 12-15) and the Las Vegas Regional (April 16-19).
